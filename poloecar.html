Powerful outside. Power-packed inside.

The Volkswagen Polo Connect Edition looks as formidable as it drives. Its built like every other Volkswagen car - sturdy, strong and precise! Right from its sporty,
yet premium exteriors to clean and ergonomic interiors, everything in the Polo Connect Edition is crafted for one thing only - your comfort.





A powerful statement...

The Polo balances its rugged design with tasteful design choices on its exterior and interior. Small details, from the subtle chrome highlights on the front grille to brushed aluminium finishes on the dashboard, make the Polo looks understated, yet premium.





Ruggedly premium

The Polo Connect Edition balances its rugged design with tasteful design choices on its exterior and interior. Small details, from the subtle chrome highlights on the front grille to brushed aluminium finishes on the dashboard,
make the Polo Connect Edition looks understated, yet premium





Your command centre

The interiors of the Polo are built with one thing in mind: your comfort. The suave leather clad flat bottom steering looks and feels nothing less than perfect. Its premium leather seating, minimal dashboard and handy controls make your journey easy and comfortable, making the Polo a pleasure to drive.




Power to you...

Style meets technology in the Polo, with the inclusion of advanced features designed to make your driving experience an absolute pleasure.
They are constantly at work, so that you can worry less about the drive and focus on the road ahead.


Rain Sensing Wipers

Ready for a splash!

Imagine a car that is always prepared for the unpredicted down pour or that light drizzle. The Polo has rain sensing wipers, that turn on automatically, when water hits your windshield. Smart? We think so!



'Climatronic' Airconditioning

Be the weatherman!

Create the perfect weather inside your car with the ‘Climatronic’ automatic air conditioning. Your Polo smartly maintains the temperature inside the cabin, enabling you to make your drives as comfortable as you want.

Cruise Control

Choose the cruise life

Driving long distances has never been such a breeze! With a simple flick of the cruise control button, the Polo will own the road at a speed you choose. So go on those long, long drives that become memories you simply can't miss!
