Designed to impress the better you

The Volkswagen Vento Connect Edition represents the best of German engineering, thoughtfully crafted to compliment you. One glimpse inside and you'll see what drives people in power. A seamlessly designed dashboard that merges with the luxurious interiors and stylish black seats that are fit for the ones in power.





Shows character.

The Vento has been designed to have a character of its own. It's luxurious without screaming out luxury. It's stylish without trying too hard. It's sophisticated and elegant, just like you. Its solid shape and distinct lines convey a sense of maturity that comes with years of bettering oneself.





Inspires confidence

Everything inside the Vento has been thought through, to the last detail. It's filled with helpful conveniences at your fingertip to make driving pleasurable. From the LED headlamps with daytime running lights, ergonomic design, plush seats to the convenient rear AC vents - everything exits for one reason - your comfort.

From the LED headlamps with daytime running lights, ergonomic design, plush seats to the convenient rear AC vents - everything exits for one reason - your comfort.





Effortlessly stylish

Style comes easy to the Vento. With simple hints of chrome and additions like the beautifully elegant LED headlamps and dynamically stylish tail lamps, the Vento looks effortlessly luxurious. Add to that the solid Volkswagen build quality and eye for precision, and you have a solid package!.




Built to outclass...

The Vento is as advanced as you when it comes to technology. It has the best of features, that work seamlessly to make your drives fun and effortless.


Cooled Glovebox

Cool it!

We all feel like having a refreshing sip or two of water or a chilled drink when on a long drive. Why settle for room temperature when you can use the glovebox of your Vento to get your drink as you want it- cold! The glovebox is actively cooled and is your personal mini-fridge.



App Connect

Hands free connectivity

Let nothing come in your way while you drive not even your smartphone. The app connect feature in the new Vento allows you to run your applications from your phone directly on to the multi function display. Thus letting you access and use music, mapping, and other apps on your dash from your compatible smartphone.

Auto Dimming Rear View Mirror

Glare no more...

The Vento is designed to make your drives effortless, at every time of the day. Or night. It thus comes with a smart rear view mirror which automatically reduces the intensity of glares from trailing vehicles. So you can drive on, with eyes on the road ahead.






Drives as easy as it is on the eyes

The Vento makes driving a pleasure like never before. With advanced technologies like DSG or the handy rear parking camera, once you own the Vento, you'll never look behind!


The smart automatic.

Our proprietary Dual Shift Gear system (DSG) is a smart automatic.
It smartly predicts your next move and pre-engages the relevant gear. This ensures uber smooth, fast shifts and amazing fuel efficiency.





As sturdy as your resolve

Along with the proprietary Volkswagen build the Vento also comes with a plethora of technologies to make your drives the safest possible. So nothing can distract you while you drive.

Hill-hold Function

Always in control.

When you resume driving on any steep incline, the hill-hold function prevents your Vento from rolling backwards.



Airbags and ABS standard.

No compromise on safety

The Vento has dual front airbags and Anti-lock Braking System as standard. Because safety can never be an option.



Electronic Stabilisation Programme

Always on track.

The ESP along with ABS will ensure that you are always in control, on slippery roads and emergency braking scenarios.




Power under the hood


The Volkswagen Vento comes with two engine options, both designed for a perfect balance between power and efficiency.
