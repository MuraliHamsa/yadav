Cutting edge.

The Tiguan is equipped with cutting edge technology and features to make your drives fun and adventures grand! This is one drive you'll always remember...





4MOTION

Adapts to your adventure

4MOTION is a smart 4-wheel drive system that actively monitors your journey. Aimed at giving you the best traction always, it dynamically controls the correct amount of torque transfer, based on the road condition.



Drive Mode Selector

Go anywhere you like

No matter where your adventures take you, the Tiguan can adapt with the Drive Mode Selector. It consists of four modes - Normal, Snow, Offroad and Offroad Individual, which change the way the Tiguan handles different terrain. .



Easy Open and Easy Close Boot

Almost magic

Have your hands full? All you have to do is move a foot in front of the sensor, and the Easy Open Boot does the work for you. Then use the Easy Close function, that automatically shuts the boot as you move away from it. It's that simple!





Carry the sky with you.


The first thing that you'll notice about the Tiguan is the big, beautiful panoramic sunroof that offers beautiful vistas above!




Spellbinding views welcome.


The Panoramic Sunroof in the Tiguan opens up to beautiful views of the heavens. It is big and wide, giving the impression of a truly open top whilst you sit comfortably inside!





Safer than safest.

The Volkswagen Tiguan is chock full of safety oriented features and technology. Built with signature Volkswagen manufacturing, the Tiguan is safer than the safest!




Airbags, ABS and ESP

Safety, Above all

Airbags reduce the risk of severe head and chest injuries in collisions with a certain severity. Optimum protection can only be achieved if the occupants are wearing their seat belts properly, as the airbags and the seat belt tensioners together form a finely tuned safety system.*



Hill Hold and Hill Descent Control

For the ups and downs

On steep inclines, Hill Hold mitigates chances of rolling down as you continue from a resting position. On downward slopes, Hill Descent Control takes control for a safe drive down.



Active Hood

Safety, extended

The Active Hood Sensor is for pedestrian safety. In case of a collision, the front bumper signals the Active Hood and raises it, minimizing damage






Ready for anything!


The Volkswagen Tiguan is built to go anywhere your adventure takes you



Pushes you, for more!

With a torque of 340 Nm, the turbocharged diesel engine is designed to perform, and propels you towards any adventure you desire.
