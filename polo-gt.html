1.2L TSI engine. Maximum power. Minimum consumption.

Engines should be about getting more, not settling for less. The Polo GT TSI’s revolutionary 1.2L engine strikes a fine balance between both, power and efficiency. It dominates with a 105PS and 175Nm torque. And, as if that isn't a mouthful, it even boasts of a 7-speed DSG (Dual-shift Gearbox) transmission.

You'd think that all this power would gobble a lot of fuel, but you'd be wrong. At 17.21 km/l*, it's just a sip. It gets you through more kilometres, with lesser emissions too.

Complementing this breakthrough internal framework is the superior build quality of Volkswagen. Trust us, it won’t cost you the earth.

The revolutionary 1.5L TDI engine.

A 1.5L TDI engine. A 5-speed gearbox. 110PS and 250Nm torque. When your car has power this formidable, it shows. And makes for a performance that Indian roads don’t see quite often. With this untameable power comes a surprisingly commanding torque. The result is an engine that makes its presence felt a whole deal faster. It doesn't take much either. With fuel consumption at 21.49 km/l*, it means more efficiency, lesser emissions and a happy conscience too. Whether it’s fuel-efficiency, power, stability or the sheer thrill of driving, we engineer our cars so that you can enjoy a world-class Volkswagen experience. And do your bit for the environment as well.

Cruise Control

Cruising on the highway is more pleasurable than ever. For your driving benefit, we’ve added the Cruise Control feature that allows you to maintain a steady speed without any added effort. Long drive, anyone?

7-speed Automatic DSG Transmission

When it comes to industry benchmarks, we’d rather set some of our own. One drive with the 7-speed Dual-shift Gearbox (available in the Polo GT TSI) and you’ll be a believer too. The automatic transmission and smooth gear handling in this refined piece of technology promises an exhilarating performance and unforgettable drive. Seventh heaven, anyone?
